The Cybersecurity Campaign Playbook provides plain language, no-nonsense practices that political campaigns around the world can use to reduce the likehood and impact of cybersecurity incidents. The Playbook has been developed in partnership between D4D Coalition members NDI and IRI in partnership with Harvard's Belfer Center and is available in a growing array of langauges.
